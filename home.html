Delivering top talent efficiently through In-house recrutiment soloutions

Located in Aberdeen Peopletraction offers a range of recruitment services to startup, SME and large businesses located in the UK.

Our recruitment In-house and RPO services aim to deliver significant cost savings and improved quality when compared to the 3rd party recruitment agency model.

Recrutment Process Outsourcing

Want to focus on delivering to your clients? The Peopletraction RPO model will define the full recruitment process, strategy, employment brand development and then deliver these services to your business based from the PeopleTraction office.

In-house recruitment soloutions

A Strategy Consultant will work with your business leaders to define a full recruitment strategy and then PeopleTraction will provide an In-house recruitment team to deliver these services.

Training

At the core of all of Peopletraction services is recruitment excellence.The training we provide to our recruiters is also available to our clients recruitment team. The training services are bespoke to each client and can encompass the full recruitment lifecycle.
