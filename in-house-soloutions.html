In-House Recruitment Soloutions

From the beginning a Strategy Consultant will work with your business leaders to define a full recruitment strategy. This strategy will define the methods your business uses to attract talent, how you brand is promoted on these channels and how your ever growing talent pool is managed to provide the maximum benefit in the future.

Once your recruitment strategy is defined, if required, People Traction will provide a team of recruiters to work in your business who take ownership for the delivery of your strategy. These recruiters remain the employees of People Traction and the size of the recruitment team can flex to suit the needs of your business. Our recruitment teams can range in size from a single part-time recruiter to a full sized recruitment function suitable for a larger organisation.

As employees of People Traction your recruiters will have continual access to the wider People Traction network ensuring they have the assistance of the strategy consultants, support of their fellow recruiters and ongoing training from People Traction providing them with all the skills they require to make your recruitment strategy a success.

Use In-house Soloutions for

Start-up

companies often need to grow their teams to realise the required growth for future funding rounds or simply to grow their business in line with their strategy. People Traction will provide a strategy consultant and recruitment team on a flexible basis allowing you to see the benefits of using specialist recruitment services without the burden of "percentage of salary" style recruitment fees.

SME

size companies can struggle to justify employing a recruitment team and the associated cost of doing so. People Traction can provide a recruiter or recruitment team on a part-time basis for your business ensuring you do not need to commit to the cost of employing a full-time recruitment team.

Large organisations

often require a continual flow of new employees to satisfy operational and business support requirements. Our recruitment strategy and recruitment teams will allow you to attract and pipeline talent to make sure you can continually provide the best services possible to your clients.

Many businesses experience periods of increase demand driven by

project specific

requirements. People Traction will create and deliver a recruitment strategy based upon the requirements of your project. Engaging our services for specific projects will ensure you can deliver services to your clients without the burden of engaging 3rd party recruitment agencies or increasing the size of your recruitment team.
